What is Groupify?

Groupify lets you and your friends make reservations together. It remembers everyone's dining preferences and habits for a more personalized dining experience.
Join A Reservation

How Is It Socially Graceful?

Our karma system uniquely rewards users who show up to their reservations. It also tracks naughty behavior such as cancelling last minute so that you don't have to be the bad guy. We'll be your karma police.

Get Group Deals

The more you show up, the more karma you gain. Get access to exclusive seats, deals, and preferences at your favorite places.

Join & Cancel Notifications

When your friends join or cancel, the group gets notified. Joins generate positive karma while cancellations cause a negative balance.

Digitally Store Bills

When you're done, Groupify lets you snap and store a photo of the bill so the group can keep it for reference.
